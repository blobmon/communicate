What is this site all about?
{{header.site_name}} is a place where one can discuss about any topic related to life in Bangalore. The site is designed keeping in mind that communication between human beings is the most important thing that can happen on the internet (or in real life).

Users can create a thread and discuss about any topic related to life in bangalore: be it discussing about things, asking for help, informing or organising events, or any other topic. The whole point is to communicate with fellow city dwellers (or non-city dwellers as long as the context is related to life in Bangalore) in an effective, direct manner.

Posting guidelines :
Do not post vulgar content, be it verbal or non-verbal. Avoid spamming and keep the content qualitative. Also, do not use this site as a mere form of mindless entertainment. There are lots of other places on the internet where one can have that kind of junk entertainment. This does not mean there cannot be any fun on this site, There can be lots. There can also be disagreement between the users since it is part of communication as long as it is done in good faith. To put it in other words, use the site to communicate with fellow humans like you would with a friend.

Information on how the site functions :
The site will not track you and will not store your IP address. The site is designed frugally to be fast and clutter free. The site will hold 200 threads at most and any new threads created will automatically delete the older-most thread (based on bump order) to keep the content fresh and dynamic.

To keep moderation of this site honest and transparent, moderation log is made public.
